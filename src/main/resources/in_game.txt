usage: eriantys <cmd>
Available commands
 /a, /assistant <id>           play an assistant card
 /sd, /dining c=<color>        move a student to dining room
 /si, /island <id> c=<color>   move a student to an island
 /m, /mother <id>              move Mother Nature to an island
 /cl, /cloud <id>              choose a cloud tile
 /ch, /character <id>          play an assistant card
